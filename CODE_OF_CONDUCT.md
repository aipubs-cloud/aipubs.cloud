# AIpubs.cloud Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

AIpubs.cloud is committed to creating an open, respectful, and intellectually
honest environment for collaboration.

---

# Expected Behavior

Community members should:

- Treat others respectfully
- Encourage constructive discussion
- Provide evidence-based feedback
- Respect different viewpoints
- Prioritize scientific integrity

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not permitted:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Deliberate misinformation
- Plagiarism
- Manipulation of research results
- Discrimination
- Malicious behavior

---

# Scientific Integrity

Contributors are expected to:

- Represent work honestly
- Properly cite sources
- Disclose conflicts of interest
- Avoid fabricated data

---

# Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when violations
occur.

Serious violations may result in permanent exclusion.

---

# Reporting

Reports should be submitted privately through the security or moderation
channels described in this repository.
